Welcome! I am an associate professor in the School of Computer Science @ Northwestern Polytechnic University. Our lab is directed by Prof. Zhiwen Yu and Prof. Bin Guo.

I received my Ph.D. degree from the School of Telecommunications Engineering @ Xidian University. I was a postdoc fellow in the EIE department @ PolyU and the School of Computer Science @ NWPU.

Research Interests

  • Foundations: Human-AI Computing Theory, Integrated AI and Network (CrowdNet).

  • Applications: Vehicular Networks, Drones, Human-Machine Collaboration.
