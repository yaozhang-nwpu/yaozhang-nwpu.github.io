Biography

Yao Zhang received his Ph.D. degree in Telecommunication Engineering from Xidian University, Xi'an, China, in 2020. He joined the School of Computer Science at Northwestern Polytechnical University, Xi'an, China, as a Post-Doctoral Researcher in 2021 and is currently an Associated Professor. He was a Research Assistant and Post-Doctoral Fellow with The Hong Kong Polytechnic University in 2019 and 2021, respectively. His research interests are in the area of ubiquitous computing, mobile crowd sensing, human-machine computing, and networked autonomous driving.
